         ↓

Body Large:   text-lg → text-xl       (18-20px)  [EMPHASIS]
              font-light

Body:         text-[15px] → text-base (15-16px)  [NORMAL]

Small:        text-sm → text-xs       (14-12px)  [SECONDARY]
